Web development in Stockholm

You can e.g. get help from us when you need a brand new site and want help with all the steps that come after your own idea. We help you with everything to be able to develop your new website according to the needs you describe. We take care of project management, design and development and it usually takes place at our office in Stockholm


Maybe you have a ready-made design that you want to bring to life on the web through HTML, CSS and JavaScript. We then code the site according to your sketches and wishes.


Another common assignment for us is companies that want to hire front-end developers as a consultant for a short or long time. Today we have consultants at a variety of companies around Stockholm.


We aim to always build a modern and optimized web where it does not matter what screen size the user uses. Based on the needs, we choose to code everything from scratch or use a CMS such as e.g.


Wordpress, Drupal or Contentful. For us, it is also important that the code that is developed has a good structure so that it can be further developed by others than those who created it.
